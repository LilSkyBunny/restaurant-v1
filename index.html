About Chang's House!

Welcome to Chang`s House. A combination of Chinese and Japanese dishes make our menu something special. Our house special of Creamy Shrimp with Walnuts, is jumbo shrimp covered with Japanese corn flakes, fried until golden crispy, topped with honey walnuts and served with pink aromatic cream sauce is a gentle mixture of earthy and floral flavors. Spicy basil chicken, a sliced chicken breast sauteed with sweet roasted peppers in a fresh Thai basil is a spicy favorite. And our Szechuan wontons in peanut sauce is a delicious balance of spice and nutty flavor. Delivery is available. Order online.
